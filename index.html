Information About me

Of my many roles over the past 15+ years, my true passion is in the 3D terrain and gaming industry where I am able to combine my artistic abilities and interests with a strong desire to innovate and solve complex problems. I also enjoy my voluntarily role as a career mentor at Booz Allen where I guide junior level mentees via regular meetings and advice sessions. This has allowed me to share my knowledge and best practices to incoming analysts as they launch their careers. I currently support a GIS team as a Data Scientist in a geospatial analyst capacity within a team of interdisciplinary leaders. I engage in ongoing global data efforts to identify, capture, build, share, and disseminate the best available structured and unstructured geospatial foundation data. I also have several years of experience as a 3D terrain developer, crafting geospecific terrains for use in simulations. This exciting work involved much attention to detail, and an in-depth knowledge of client expectations in creating purpose-built terrain. Throughout my 14+ year career, the past 10 years at Booz Allen have been particularly rewarding as I have been able to work for a variety of different clients including the NGA, DHS, and the U.S. Army. Prior to joining the firm, I worked in the commercial digital mapping industry at NAVTEQ as traffic quality analyst, and later, as a points of interest (POI) specialist. Both roles involved a keen sense of detail and QA/QC procedures which set the pace for a quality-focused career.

2023 - present

Data Scientist - Booz Allen Hamilton

In my new role as a Data Scientist (February 2023), I am working primarily in Python and Jupyter notebooks to automate process flows and manage geospatial services in the ArcGIS Online environment as part of a data management team.

2014 - 2023

Geospatial Analyst - Booz Allen Hamilton

Transformed NGA global UNCLASS datasets into ArcGIS Online geospatial products. Maintained web services and web maps of human geography data in response to global natural and manmade disasters. Curated and maintained said human geography datasets for use in future global events.

2013 - 2014

Virtual 3D Terrain Geospatial Lead - Booz Allen Hamilton

Leveraged TerraTools, ArcGIS, and QGIS to create 3D terrain models for use in the Army’s Virtual Battlespace 3 (VBS3) virtual synthetic training environment. Developed large scale (100KM x 100KM) 3D terrain models for use in Army platoon-level virtual training at Fort Bragg and in support of other installations across the U.S. Army. Incorporated road, building, vegetation, and vehicular models into terrain-builds to depict geospecific environments with real-life geologic features and physical urbanized locations.

2012 - 2013

Geospatial Information Exchange Broker - Booz Allen Hamilton

2010 - 2012

Points of Interest Specialist - HERE Maps

Part of an international data-onboarding team utilizing Agile methodologies and Jira issue-tracking and ETL data transformations.

2008 - 2010

Traffic Quality Analyst - HERE Maps
